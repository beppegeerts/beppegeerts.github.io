RADIO… (poster)

As the first project of the second year, we had to make a graphic identity for a secret radio. We had to make a playlist on a poster, 3 QSL cards and a flag. My playlist was based on the soundtracks of stalker movies: the boy next door, Hush, Fatal Attraction and Fear. I chose to take pictures of windows in my own town. In a rare and creepy way it’s a form of stalking. The flashlight on my flag represents the camera of the stalker.
